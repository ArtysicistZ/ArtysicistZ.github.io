The Franklink Experience

From your first message to a thriving professional network, all through iMessage. No app download. No sign-up form. Just text.

Back to Technical Details
Onboarding
01

Meet Your AI Concierge

Frank feels like texting a friend. The AI concierge learns your background, interests, and career goals through natural, casual conversation.

02

Secure Login & Privacy

Privacy-first onboarding with read-only email access via Composio OAuth. Frank scans your professional context without storing sensitive data.

Supporting
03

Define Your Goals

Tell Frank what you're looking for — internship advice, co-founders, or mentors. The system captures demand and value signals for semantic matching.

04

Showcase Your Skills

Share your projects and experience. Frank extracts value signals and stores them as embeddings — so others can find you by what you bring to the table.

Networking
05

Connection Requests

Frank delivers personalized introductions right to your iMessage inbox. People who want to connect reach you through AI-curated messages — no app needed.

06

Grow Your Network

As new members join, Frank broadcasts relevant introductions and saves contacts. Your network grows organically with real-time community announcements.

Group Formation
07

Smart Group Matching

Ask Frank to find more people with shared interests — and it creates multi-person group chats with AI-generated icebreakers powered by the Zep knowledge graph. The first chat that becomes your startup.
